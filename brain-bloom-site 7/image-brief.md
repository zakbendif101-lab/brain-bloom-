# Brain Bloom Education — Image Generation Brief

**For use with:** Nano Banana (Google Gemini Image Generation)

**How to use this document:**
1. Open Nano Banana
2. Copy the **Prompt** for each image, paste it in
3. Generate, regenerate if needed (sometimes 2–3 attempts get the best result)
4. Save as the **suggested filename** so I can wire it in directly
5. Send the images back to me when done

**Important — global style rules to keep the brand consistent:**

These apply to every image. Nano Banana respects style descriptions when they're at the start of the prompt, so the prompts below all begin with these.

- Shot on a **Sony A7 IV with a 35mm or 50mm prime lens** (for documentary realism)
- Natural light, never harsh
- Slightly desaturated, warm colour grade — not over-saturated stock-photo brightness
- **British setting cues:** primary school uniforms (navy, grey, white shirts), standard exercise books, classroom equipment that looks UK-standard not American
- **Children appear from behind, at distance, in profile, or with hands/materials in focus** — never identifiable close-up faces. This protects safeguarding AND looks more premium.
- Avoid: glowing brain icons, lightbulbs over heads, floating books, "happy diverse classroom" stock poses, oversaturated colours, cartoon styles

## After you generate these

When you've got the images, send them to me one at a time or in a batch with the filenames. I'll:

1. Compress each one for web (so the site loads fast)
2. Wire them into the right sections of each page

6. Re-zip the site for you to drop onto Netlify

## Quality bar — when to regenerate

Reject and regenerate if any image has:
- ❌ Visible faces of children that look identifiable
- ❌ That AI "smoothness" — skin too perfect, surfaces too clean
- ❌ Oversaturated colours (the dead giveaway of AI slop)
- ❌ Weird hands (extra fingers, melted-looking objects)
- ❌ American-looking school environment (lockers, US flags, distinctive US uniforms)
- ❌ Cartoonish or illustrated style when documentary was specified
- ❌ Floating objects, glowing effects, lightbulb metaphors

You're aiming for: "this could be a real magazine editorial about education in the UK." If it looks like a stock-photo site, regenerate.
